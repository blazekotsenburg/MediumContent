Spam & Scams
Unsolicited bulk messaging, phishing links, pyramid schemes, or fraudulent solicitations. Social engineering to take advantage of another person.

Self‑harm
Any message that encourages, describes, or supports self-injury, suicide, or harming oneself or others. This includes telling someone to hurt themselves or sharing methods for self-harm.

Safe
The message does not break any of the listed policies. It is respectful, appropriate, and does not raise any safety or moderation concerns.

Privacy & Personal Data Exposure
Sharing someone’s private information without permission. This includes addresses, phone numbers, email addresses, financial info, or anything that could identify someone personally.
